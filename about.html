HANK AND COMPANY


Everyday is an adventure and Hank doesn't want to miss out on a single thing. The biggest adventures are those he shares with his friends. He met each of them over the last few years, and just happened to be looking for some roommates, and each of them were looking for a new place to move to. As the saying goes, 'The more, the merrier'. Hank wants to share his adventures of his friends and family to any out there that also loves adventure as much as he does.


Adventures can be big or small, as long as you are trying something new - it can be an adventure! I have learned being a home-owner, is in and of itself, an adventure. It can be challenging and fun, but also frustrating and rewarding when you can make something better than it was. I have also found an adventure doesn't have to be exhilarating, it can just be relaxing on a beach in a new place, or even one that is familiar. An adventure is what you make of it, and helps you live life to the fullest. I can't wait to see how much my albums grow over the years, and the new memories and friends I make.
